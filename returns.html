Our Return Policy

CampSupply is committed to quality and 100% customer satisfaction. If you are not satisfied with your order, you may return the product for a refund of the purchased price
If you are not satisfied with your order, the follwing requirements must be followed for returning merchandise:
      The client must submit a written return request within 48 hours of the coaching session.
      The written return must clearly explain that the coaching session did not provide any new insights or guidance on how to move forward in the specific area covered.
      The return applies to the single coaching session fee only and does not cover the entire coaching program.
      In the event that two return requests are made within any 12–month period, both requests will be honoured under the terms above, but the coaching agreement will be terminated.
