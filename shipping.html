Our Shipping Policy


Order Processing

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days.
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your order has been processed.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard 3–7 business days Calculated at checkout
Expedited 2–3 business days Calculated at checkout
Priority 1–2 business days Calculated at checkout

Delivery delays may occur during peak seasons or due to carrier issues.


Shipment Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Please allow up to 24 hours for tracking updates to appear.


Shipping Restrictions

  • We currently ship only within the UK.
  • We do not ship to P.O. Boxes or APO/FPO addresses.
  • Some oversized or heavy items may not qualify for all shipping methods.
